Release Date: April 18, 2025
Duration: 135 minutes
Language: Hindi
Genre: Historical Drama

Synopsis

Justice Chettoor Sankaran Nair takes a stand against the British Empire after uncovering the truth about the Jallianwala Bagh massacre. Fighting in court with the help of a young revolutionary and a student, he challenges the lies of General Dyer and the empire itself. This courtroom drama uncovers the hidden truths of colonial injustice.
